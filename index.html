The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa , on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India. Originally a private art collection of the Salar Jung family, it was endowed to the nation after the death of Salar Jung III. It was inaugurated on 16 December 1951.It has a collection of sculptures, paintings and furniture from Japan and North America. It is one of the largest museums in the world.
