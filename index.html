salarjung museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa,on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India. It was inaugurated on 16 December 1951.It has a collection of sculptures, paintings and furniture from Japan and North America. It is one of the largest museums in the world.
