Kate and Willie

Kate and Willy-Hofstra University's costumed lion mascots.

  1. Kate and Willie, Hofstra's lion and lioness mascot duo, have been representing the Pride for over 20 years now.
  2. The sight of the two lions high-fiving and greeting students has become a welcomed part of the Hofstra culture.
  3. The two lions were then named Kate and Willie in honor of the founders of the university, Kate and William Hofstra.
